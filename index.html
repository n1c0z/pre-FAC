ABOUT ME

Web development is becoming my greatest passion for a variety of reasons.

To start with, it embodies creativity, innovation, critical thinking, logic and most importantly engagement and interaction to its core. What I love the most about it is the sense of fulfilment and satisfaction it creates. The possibility of creating something that can improve a process or someone'92s way of work or life, create something new or offer an alternative solution to a problem is my biggest aspiration for learning web design.

Even though I am still on my first steps, I have thoroughly enjoyed the journey so far and I am looking forward to continue, not on my own anymore, but as part of a collaborative community, such as Founders and Coders.

WORK EXPERIENCE

My first steps in web design started as a side project in my current job at St George's Hospital Tooting.

Being part of a team that constantly collects data to monitor adherence to several quality standards, it became necessary to have a centralised database where all these data are stored. On top of that, members of staff who where involved in the data collection process needed to interact with this database and check the progress of their projects. With a limited budget and lack of proprietary software, developing a website that links to a database became our only way forward.

After a year in the making and in between other projects we finally have a functional site that aims to serve this purpose. However, as with most web design projects, there is room for improvement.

I strongly believe the Founders and Coders community can assist greatly in moving this project forward.
