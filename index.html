WORK EXPERIENCE

My first steps in web deisgn started as a side project in my current job at St George's Hospital Tooting.

Being part of a team that constantly collects data to monitor adherance to several quality standards, it became mecessary to have a cetralised database where all these data are stored. On top of that, members of staff who where innvolved in the data collection process needed to interact with this database and check the progress of their projects. With a limited budget and lack of proprierty software, developing a website that links to a database became our only way forward.

After a year in the making and in between other projects we finally have a functional site that aims to serve this purpose. However, as with the majority of web design projects, there is always room for improvement.

I am hoping that the Founders and Coders community and the opportunity to participate in the full stack developer course, will assist greatly in moving this project forward.
